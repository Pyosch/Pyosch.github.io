<h3>Regionale Virtuelle Kraftwerke (VISE)</h3>
<p class="project-meta">Virtual Institute Smart Energy · 2017 – 2020</p>

Research on regional virtual power plants and their role in the future energy system. The project explored business models, aggregation concepts, and technical integration of decentralized energy resources at the household level into virtual power plants operating within distribution grids.

<div class="project-card">
<h3>INBIS — Bidirectional Use of Industrial Battery Storage</h3>
<p class="project-meta">TH Köln · Research project</p>

Investigation of bidirectional utilization of industrial battery energy storage systems. The project focused on enabling battery storage in industrial settings to provide grid services and participate in flexibility markets, going beyond their primary use case.

<h3>VISE-D Smart Data</h3>
<p class="project-meta">Virtual Institute Smart Energy · Data-driven energy research</p>

A follow-up initiative within the Virtual Institute Smart Energy framework, focusing on smart data approaches for the energy sector — leveraging data analytics and digital tools to improve decision-making in distributed energy systems.

<div class="project-card">
<h3>Innovationspark Erneuerbare Energien Jüchen (IEE)</h3>
<p class="project-meta">Zweckverband LANDFOLGE Garzweiler · Concept study · 2021</p>

Concept study for an innovation park for renewable energies in Jüchen, developed in the context of structural change in the Rhenish mining district. The study explored how former mining areas can be transformed into hubs for renewable energy innovation.
